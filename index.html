About Me
Hello! My name is Carley. I earned my BA in Religion and English from UF in 2020. I am soon to graduate with my MS in Management and a certificate in Web Design from UF this month.

Rigby

One interest I have is that I love animals. I have had many pets throughout the years, such as dogs, cats, turtles, and ducks! My current pet cat is named Rigby, and I found him in a dumpster. He is so sweet and loving, you would never know he was born in the wild.

Alberta

My favorite activity to do is my role on the Florida Gators mascot team. I have been on the team for 4 years, getting to perform as Alberta the Gator all over the country. I love meeting all the Gators fans and especially kids. I will miss this very much when I graduate this month, but I am hoping to continue working in the mascot industry professionally.

Manatee

My favorite animal is the manatee. I was born and raised in Manatee County, Florida. We were home to the oldest living manatee in the world, Snooty, who lived to be 69 until an untimely accidental death. I love the Florida wilderness and waterways, and I hope to help in any way I can to keep the manatee population healthy and strong.
